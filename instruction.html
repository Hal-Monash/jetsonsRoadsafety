Quiz Instruction

In this section, there are a total of 10 quizzes waiting for you.

For each quiz, there is only one right answer.

The question will be shown on the top right of each page.

For each quiz, the choices can be found like the following images

Name ChoiceA ChoiceB
Car
Lane

You can make your choice by moving the cursor:

(which can be found in the middle of the screen, like the shape of steering wheel) to “CAR” or “LINE” you think has the right of the way.

  • If your choice is right, you will move on to the next quiz until the end.
  • If your choice is wrong, there will also be a pop window to remind you.

You can always quit by clicking “BACK” bottom.
